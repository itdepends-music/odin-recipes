Nanowave Pulse Generator

Description

Due to the unavailability of temporal anomalies, psyphonic mutation has been attempted in some individuals to allow energizing via nanowave pulse generators. The process of using a nanowave pulse is involved and complicated, hence the existence of this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 Gigawatt Energy Conduits
  • 3 Nanowave Pulse Beams
  • 7 Sidefumbling Prevention Arms
  • 1 Micro Encabulator (Can be replaced with older Turbo Encabulator in a pinch)

Steps

  1. Use the first energy conduit to power up the micro encabulator.
  2. Utilize the 7 sidefumbling prevention arms in sockets 7, 9, and 13.
  3. Connect nanowave pulse beams.
  4. Connect nanowave pulse beams to the second gigawatt energy conduit.
