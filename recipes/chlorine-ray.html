Chlorine Ray

Description

Chlorine rays were once a delicacy for the Odins, representing the finest delicacy possible. However this changed after the discovery of SYH-027485, a dimension consisting entirely of chlorine. The Chlorine cartel tried to prevent knowledge of this dimension from entering the general population, however the effort was mostly futile. Modern day Odin's regard the food as disgusting, however the food is still used in Odin rituals due to it's prior status as a product of luxury. This has been a continued barrier for Odin's attempting to increase their social cast.

Ingredients

  • 10 High Value Analyzers
  • 7 Optical Waneshafts
  • 3000 kg Liquefied Chlorine
  • 1 g Catylitic Assemblers

Steps

  1. Insert the liquefied chlorine into a vyelf converter.
  2. Slowly insert the catylitic assemblers over 1 hour.
  3. Insert waneshafts. These should begin to assemble immediately.
  4. Now add the high value analyzers. Be careful to ensure that there are never more then two anassembled analyzers at a time.
